01 About me

I help brands find their voice and wear it with confidence — through systems that are beautiful, considered and built to scale.

For nearly a decade I’ve partnered with startups, cultural institutions and consumer brands to shape identities that feel inevitable. My practice spans the full arc of a brand — from the first sketch and naming workshop to packaging on the shelf, the campaign in the feed, and the product in your hands.

I believe great design is equal parts strategy and craft. Every grid, glyph and gradient should earn its place. The result is work that doesn’t just look good in a case study — it performs in the wild.

A joint-savings app for couples and friends saving toward shared goals.
